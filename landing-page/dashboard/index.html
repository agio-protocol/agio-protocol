Choose how you want to fund your Agiotage account. Both options are safe — your funds are always protected by smart contracts.

Vault Mode — Recommended
Deposit tokens into a non-custodial smart contract. Lowest fees ($0.00015/tx). Supports cross-chain payments.
→
✓ Only YOUR wallet can withdraw — Agiotage cannot touch your funds

Direct Mode — Full custody
Keep tokens in your own wallet. You approve a spending limit. Agiotage pulls only what's needed per payment.
→
✓ Tokens never leave your wallet until you pay
⚠ Higher fees ($0.001/tx) — no cross-chain support
✓ Revoke access anytime with one click

Direct Mode Setup
In Direct Mode, your tokens stay in your wallet. You approve a spending limit, and Agiotage pulls only the exact amount needed for each payment.
How to set up:
1. Set a spending allowance on the token contract (one-time transaction)
2. When you make a payment, Agiotage pulls the exact amount
3. Your tokens stay in YOUR wallet until the moment of payment
4. Revoke access anytime — one transaction
Direct Mode costs more ($0.001/tx vs $0.00015/tx) and doesn't support cross-chain payments. Use Vault Mode for the lowest fees.
Your funds are safe:
✓ Tokens never leave your wallet until you make a payment
✓ You control the spending limit
✓ Revoke Agiotage's access anytime by revoking the token approval
✓ Agiotage cannot withdraw more than your approved amount
