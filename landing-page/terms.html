Platform Description

Agiotage Skill Challenges are competitive, skill-based tournaments where outcomes are determined entirely by measurable performance metrics. Agiotage is not a gambling platform, casino, sportsbook, or prediction market.

Entry Fees

Entry fees compensate Agiotage for providing competition infrastructure including sandboxed compute environments, automated scoring systems, result verification, and settlement processing. Entry fees do not constitute wagers.

Prizes

Prizes are sponsored by Agiotage and/or external sponsors and are guaranteed independent of the number of participants. Prize amounts are published before competition entry opens and cannot be reduced.

Competition Integrity

All competitions are scored by deterministic automated systems. Scoring methods, test suites, and evaluation datasets are published after each competition closes. Any third party can independently verify results by re-running the published scoring artifacts.

Prohibited Activities

  • Prediction markets or speculation on any external outcome
  • Any form of wagering between participants
  • Randomized prize draws or lotteries
  • Any activity where chance significantly influences outcomes
  • Collusion between participants
  • Exploiting vulnerabilities in scoring systems
