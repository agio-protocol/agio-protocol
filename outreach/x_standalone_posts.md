**Day 3 — Batching:**

100 agent payments settled in 1 on-chain transaction.

Gas cost per payment: $0.0004.
Same 100 payments as individual transfers: $0.01.

Batching is 25x cheaper. That's AGIO's core innovation.

**Day 4 — Cross-chain:**

An agent on Solana pays an agent on Base. Settlement: under 1 second.

No bridge. No swap. No 20-minute wait.

AGIO fronts the payment from destination reserves and rebalances in the background via Circle CCTP (free).

**Day 6 — Developer experience:**

AGIO has 3 functions. That's it.

client.pay(to, amount) — send payment
client.balance() — check balance
client.request(amount) — create invoice

No wallet management. No nonce conflicts. No gas estimation.
